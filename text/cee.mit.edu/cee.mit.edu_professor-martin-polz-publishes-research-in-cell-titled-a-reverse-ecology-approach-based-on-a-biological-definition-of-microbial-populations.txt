» Professor Martin Polz publishes research in Cell titled “A reverse ecology approach based on a biological definition of microbial populations” Published on: Aug 16, 2019 Aug 16 2019 Share  Professor Martin Polz published a new research paper in Cell titled, “A reverse ecology approach based on a biological definition of microbial populations.” The researchers have developed a new method that allows for the identification of ecologically and medically relevant microbial population structure that can help pinpoint the genetic factors associated with environmental factors as well as human diseases.

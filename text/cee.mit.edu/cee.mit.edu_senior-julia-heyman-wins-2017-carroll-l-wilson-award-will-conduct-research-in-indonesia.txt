Wilson Award, will conduct research in Indonesia Published on: Mar 07, 2017 Mar 07 2017 Share  Senior Julia Heyman was recently awarded the 2017 Caroll L. Wilson Award, a prize that funds a year of research. She will travel to Indonesia after graduation and will be pursuing a project on the implementation of wheelchair sensors. More information on Heyman’s research and the award can be read on MIT News.

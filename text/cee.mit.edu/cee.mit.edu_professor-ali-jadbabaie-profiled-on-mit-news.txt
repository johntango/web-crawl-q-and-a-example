Jadbabaie profiled on MIT News Published on: Nov 14, 2016 Nov 14 2016 Share  JR East Professor of Engineering Ali Jadbabaie was recently profiled on MIT News for his research on large-scale systems and his path to MIT.  Jadbabaie joined the CEE faculty in July and also serves as the associate director the Institute for Data, Systems, and Society (IDSS) and as the director of Sociotechnical Systems Research Center (SSRC).

of climate change and irrigation Published on: Aug 03, 2018 Aug 03 2018 Share  Research from Professor Elfatih Eltahir shows that China’s most populous and agriculturally important region could face deadly heat waves unless significant measures are taken at the global scale to limit greenhouse gas emissions, and mitigate climate change. The findings were published in Nature Communications. Read more on MIT News.
